About Us

What is ABUDevs? and how can it help me?

ABUDevs is a student developer community based in Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria that aims to help student developers reach the peak of their career by providing them with the resources that they'd need to do so.

How do I join ABUDevs? It's really easy

Joining the ABUDevs community isn't hard, all you need to do is sign up and just like that, you're part of the family.

How do I connect with ABUDevs? It's quite simple

Connecting with ABUDevs is really easy, all you need to do is send us a message via our contact form and we'll reach out to you.
